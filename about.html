About TIEG

Hello! I’m TIEG, the author of Echoes Beyond the Horizon and an app developer passionate about creating interactive web novel experiences.

This website is designed to provide an immersive reading experience, combining story, navigation, and interactive features like login, messaging, and chapter navigation.

All chapters are connected, and you can explore the story, message the author, and read securely thanks to Appwrite backend integration.

Thank you for reading! Your support means a lot.
